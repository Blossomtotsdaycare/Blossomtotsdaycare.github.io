What we do.

Blossom Tots is a premier family daycare and preschool in Warm Springs, Fremont, California. Our mission is to create a safe and healthy environment where children can flourish. With a holistic approach, we prioritize cleanliness and maintain an inviting and hygienic space. We offer stimulating activities and events that cater to various aspects of a child's development, including festive celebrations and yoga sessions for physical and mental well-being. Fitness and play are emphasized, and our outdoor play area encourages active exploration. Art and craft activities promote creative expression and fine motor skill development. Our goal is to provide an exceptional early learning experience that sets the foundation for lifelong growth. Join our warm and welcoming community, where your child's well-being and happiness are our top priorities.

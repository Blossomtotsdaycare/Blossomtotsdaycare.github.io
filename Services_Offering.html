Extracurricular Activities.

Extracurricular Activities.

Activities which your child will learn at our DayCare.

  • Blossomtots daycare also features a beautiful garden area where children can engage in outdoor play. This space not only allows them to have fun but also promotes their physical development and muscle building.

  • At Blossomtots Group play is an integral part of our daycare program. We organize various activities to promote social interaction and learning. From singing and dancing to play acting and games, children actively participate in activities that foster their social skills. We also dedicate time for reading stories, which cultivates a love for books and enhances their language abilities. To further stimulate their creativity, we provide art supplies for painting, drawing, cutting, and gluing.

  • Social play is encouraged as children interact and play with their peers. Through these interactions, they learn valuable skills such as taking turns, cooperation, and sharing. Such activities contribute to their overall social and emotional growth.

  • In addition to group and social play, we emphasize the importance of constructive play. Children are provided with drawing materials and building blocks, encouraging them to experiment and create. Music is also incorporated into their playtime, fostering their creativity and self-expression.

  • Fantasy play is another component of our program that nurtures imagination. Through role-playing games and dress-up activities, children enhance their communication skills and learn about different roles and responsibilities. Additionally, we introduce fitness-related activities like board games and hopscotch to promote physical activity and educate children about the importance of staying active.

  • At Blossom Tots, we are dedicated to providing a caring, stimulating, and engaging environment where children can learn, grow, and have fun. We strive to support their holistic development and help them acquire a wide range of skills that will benefit them throughout their lives.

  • At Blossom Tots, we offer meditation and yoga to children, providing additional benefits such as focus, concentration, emotional regulation, and stress reduction. Meditation teaches simple breathing techniques and calming exercises, promoting self-awareness and inner peace. Yoga enhances physical strength, flexibility, and balance, improving coordination and body awareness while fostering mindfulness and presence Our commitment to incorporating meditation and yoga reflects our dedication to a comprehensive and holistic approach to child care and development.

  • At Blossom Tots daycare, we believe in making every moment special for your child. That's why we host festive celebrations that create joyful memories. From holiday-themed parties to cultural festivities, we ensure that children experience the magic of celebrations while fostering a sense of community. Join us at Blossom Tots, where we celebrate and cherish the spirit of childhood.
